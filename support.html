Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I create a study session?

A: Tap the "Start" button on the home screen, set your desired duration, and tap "Start Session". You'll receive a room code to share with friends.

Q: How do I join a friend's session?

A: Tap "Join Session" on the home screen and enter the 6-digit room code your friend shared with you.

Q: What counts as a violation?

A: Leaving the app during an active study session triggers a violation. A 5-second grace period is allowed for quick tasks.

Q: How do I delete my data?

A: Go to Account History and use the "Wipe History" option to delete all your session data and reset statistics.

Q: What is Marathon Mode?

A: Marathon Mode allows you to study without a preset timer. The session continues until you manually end it.

App Features

  • Timed Sessions: Set a specific study duration from 5 minutes to several hours
  • Marathon Mode: Study without time limits
  • Social Accountability: Study with friends using room codes
  • Violation Tracking: Monitor focus and app-switching during sessions
  • Session History: Review your past study sessions and progress
  • Statistics: Track total study hours and completed sessions
